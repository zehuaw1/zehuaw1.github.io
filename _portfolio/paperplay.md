## Overview

Built at **HackMIT 2025**, winning the **2nd Place Modal Prize**. PaperPlay turns hand-drawn sketches into fully playable physics-based games in real time.

## How It Works

1. **Draw** — Sketch game elements on paper (platforms, obstacles, characters)
2. **Scan** — OpenCV processes the sketch, identifying shapes and structure
3. **Play** — A physics engine brings the sketch to life as a playable game
4. **Commentary** — Real-time AI narrates the gameplay for extra fun
